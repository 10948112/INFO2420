About Extreme Honeymoons

Extreme Honeymoons plans once-in-a-lifetime adventures for newlyweds who want more than ordinary. From glacier hikes and ocean kayaking to mountain treks, trips are tailored for comfort, safety, and unforgettable views.

Advisors work with each couple’s goals and budget to build an itinerary that blends excitement with time to relax and celebrate.

What We Do

We coordinate certified guides, reputable outfitters, and boutique stays. Each plan includes options for dining and downtime so couples can enjoy the moment while we handle the details.

Our Approach

Simple choices, clear pricing, and responsive support before, during, and after travel. The result is a smooth experience from send‑off to return.
